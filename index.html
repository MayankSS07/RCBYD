About Us


Rotract Club Of Belgaum Yuva.... As the name suggests , it simply means YOUTH IS THE MIRROR OF SOCIETY. Our motive is to positively influence a youth into action and make a small change. We make sure that all the members of the club are given equal opportunity and are acknowledged. As the saying goes "With Great Power comes Great Responsibilities", Yuva Darpan has never backed out from any responsibility and will continue to do so. Once a Darpanite always a Darpanite!

Flagship events

Why Join Rotract

Rotract is all about journey of serving the society to fellowship to personality development...Still confused what all happens in Rotract??.....Come join us for the best session, all about Rotract.

Pawtography

A photography competition on the occasion of world photography day. Where you can showcase the best profiles of your dog and win exciting prizes.

Ekdanta Chitrakala

Send your Painting/Sketches of Lord Ganesha on the Occasion of Ganesh Chaturthi and showcase us your artistic skills and win exciting prizes.

Kambali

KAMBALI is a Blanket donation drive, conducted in the winter season. We collect blankets and donation for blankets for a month and we do a donation drive for needy people. In past 3 years RCBYD has donated about 600 blankets.

Blood Donation Camp

Your little effort can give others second chance to live life. Give someone the gift of life............."YOU LIVE, WHEN YOU GIVE".Come join us for our Blood Donation Camp.

DOG Friendship Bands

"Dogs do speak, but only those who listen" RCBYD in Collaboration with Stray Care Foundation Belagavi Dog friendship band drive for our loyal friends. A Reflective Belts which protects them and makes them look stylish.
